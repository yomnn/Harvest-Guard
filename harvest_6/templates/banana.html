Sunlight And Temperature

Bananas thrive in full sun, requiring around 10 to 12 hours of direct sunlight daily for optimal growth. Adequate sunlight exposure promotes healthy leaf growth and supports banana development. Humidity

Bananas thrive in tropical climates with high humidity. They prefer humidity levels between 50% to 70%. However, good airflow is essential to prevent fungal diseases. High humidity can promote diseases like Panama disease or anthracnose, so proper airflow is crucial for disease prevention.

Water

Bananas require consistent moisture throughout their growth stages. They thrive in well-draining soil but need regular watering, especially during dry periods. Adequate irrigation is crucial, but overwatering can lead to root rot. Sufficient water availability supports healthy growth and fruit development.

Soil

Bananas prefer fertile, well-draining soil with a pH ranging between 5.5 and 7.0. Sandy loam or loamy soil types are suitable. They benefit from soil rich in organic matter as it retains moisture and provides essential nutrients. Good drainage is essential to prevent waterlogging and ensure healthy root development.
