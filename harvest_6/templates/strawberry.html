Sunlight And Temperature

Strawberries require ample sunlight for optimal growth and fruit production. They thrive in full sun conditions, needing around 6 to 8 hours of direct sunlight daily. Additionally, strawberries prefer cooler temperatures during the growing season, ideally between 60째F (16째C) and 80째F (27째C). Adequate sunlight exposure and suitable temperatures are crucial for flowering, fruit development, and the overall yield of strawberries.

Humidity

Strawberries prefer moderate humidity levels. While they can tolerate different humidity levels, excessive moisture in the air can lead to fungal diseases. Proper air circulation and well-spaced plants help mitigate humidity-related issues.

Water

Strawberries require consistent moisture, especially during fruit development. Adequate irrigation is crucial, but it's important to avoid waterlogging, which can cause root damage. Well-draining soil with controlled watering practices helps maintain optimal soil moisture levels for strawberry plants.

Soil

Strawberries thrive in well-draining, loamy soils with good fertility and a slightly acidic pH between 5.5 and 6.5. Soil rich in organic matter supports healthy root growth and overall plant health.
